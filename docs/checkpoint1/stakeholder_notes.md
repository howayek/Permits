# Stakeholder Notes (Anonymized)

- Stakeholders want the process to remain partially manual in the early stage (human review is required first), but they want less in-person paperwork and fewer repeated visits.
- We discussed this concept with a senior government stakeholder (Ministry-level) and learned that even getting a telecom phone line can involve a permit-style process with document submission and approvals.
- Key needs: clear required-doc checklist, trackable status updates, and a simple way to verify an approved permit (QR validity check).
